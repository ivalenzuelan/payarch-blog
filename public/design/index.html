Usage rules

A few rules that matter more than the hex values themselves:

  • One accent on a viewport. signal-600 is reserved for the highest-priority action.
  • Diagrams own the multi-color budget. Outside figures, the page is paper + ink + at most one accent.
  • Tag pills and metadata use the ink scale. Color costs attention; tags don't deserve it.
  • Borders are hairlines, not boxes. ink-200 at 1px is the workhorse divider.
  • Cards have either a hairline border or a soft shadow, never both.
  • Diagrams pair every color with a shape, label, or stroke pattern for grayscale legibility.
